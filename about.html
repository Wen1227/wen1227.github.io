ABOUT

LEE WEN_

I like drawing illustrations very much. Drawing what I want to draw will make me feel very happy, satisfied, and fulfilling. I like to exaggerate the expressions and body parts of the characters, and vent the pressure that has always been accumulated. Through drawing illustrations, I Find another way to relieve pressure and a sense of fulfillment that has never been seen before. I am used to taking a fixed path, but I hate this kind of unchanging life. I hope that I can continue to insist on design or painting in the future.
